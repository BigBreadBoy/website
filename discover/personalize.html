Personalize

More freedom for customisation

See how to personalize your PC in Windows 7. (4:50)

One thing people miss about Windows 7 is the freedom to express who they are— personalising their computer to themselves, one that connects with their identity.

Your computer is capable of many great things. It should reflect YOU and YOUR style too, for a comfortable and personalized environment YOU can work on.

Since then, this capability of customisation; the range of how far you can natively go with personalizing your computer to yourself has shrunk, in place with a flat interface some might enjoy, and some not.

But wouldn't it be great if you had the chance to make that computer unique to yourself, once again?

Uniqueness. Distinctive. Beautiful.

In the present, it's somewhat become way less exciting to interact with your computer reflecting the style of yours. But doesn't it feel more stylish, reflective, unique and personalized in Windows 7?

With Reloaded7, you can experience that. Once again.

The eye-catching and unique look of Windows 7 is what made it so exciting to interact with the premium glossy design of Aero. And even better, changing that premium design to how you like it.

The intuitive Start menu; the visually appealing window borders, the simplified Superbar is yours to personalize to your level.

That is what Reloaded7 aims to recreate. The visual appeal of Aero, the freedom of personalization — and the nostalgic exciting feeling of using it.
